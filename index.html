PSU Academic Accreditation System

View project on GitHub

Prince Sultan university commits to provide quality teaching and learning through a longue and tedious process, according to international standards, as per the requirements of the NCAAA national accreditation agency. Indeed, each faculty member need to provide a comprehensive course report at the end of each semester, with his observations, recommendations, summary of grades and activities, etc. Several statistics are also measured to assess the achieved learning outcomes of offered programs and courses. The reports are sent back to the colleges for analysis and decision-making.
